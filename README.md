# 🌸 Whispers Garden

> *A place to slow down, pick up a brush, and leave a little piece of yourself blooming in a garden that grows with every whisper.*

**Whispers Garden** is a peaceful, browser-based creative experience where art meets reflection. Paint your own flower, attach a quiet thought to it, and plant it in a living garden of whispers.

---

## 🌿 What is it?

Pick up a virtual brush, mix soft pastel colours, and paint your own flower — petal by petal, stroke by stroke. When your flower feels ready, attach a *whisper* to it: a small thought, a quiet wish, a feeling you've been carrying, or a word of kindness for someone you love.

Then plant it. Watch it bloom alongside every flower that came before it.

Whispers Garden is for the days when you need to slow down. For the thoughts that don't quite fit into a conversation. For the small, tender feelings that deserve somewhere to live.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🖌️ **Drawing Studio** — freehand brush, flood fill, eraser, and decorative stamps (petals, leaves, dots, stars)
- 🎨 **Colour Palette** — 15 curated pastel colours + a custom colour picker and opacity control
- ↩️ **Undo / Redo** — full history support (also works with Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y)
- 💬 **Whispers** — attach a short message, wish, or feeling to your flower before planting 
- 🌱 **Living Garden** — a growing space where all your flowers sway gently and reveal their whispers on hover
- 🔊 **Ambient Sound** — optional soft wind/nature sound generated in-browser
- 📱 **Responsive** — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

No installation required. Whispers Garden runs entirely in your browser as a single HTML file.

### Option 1 — Run locally
1. Download or clone this repository
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. It opens in your browser — start painting 🌸

### Option 2 — Play online
Visit the live site:
**[khushibhavsar.github.io/whispers-garden](https://khushibhavsar.github.io/whispers-garden/)**

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5 Canvas** — for the drawing studio
- **CSS3** — animations, glassmorphism, responsive layout
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — all logic, flood fill, undo/redo, and garden management
- **Web Audio API** — for the ambient wind sound (no audio files needed)
- **Google Fonts** — Cormorant Garamond + Lato

No frameworks. No dependencies. No build steps. Just one file.

## 🎮 How to Use

1. **Studio tab** — choose a tool and colour, then draw your flower on the canvas
2. Click **"Plant this flower →"** when you're happy with it
3. Type a whisper (or plant quietly without one)
4. Switch to the **Garden tab** to see your flower swaying in the garden
5. Hover over any flower to reveal its whisper
6. Toggle 🔊 ambient sound in the bottom-right corner

### Keyboard Shortcuts
| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Ctrl + Z` | Undo |
| `Ctrl + Y` | Redo |

---

## 💡 Tips

- Use the **fill tool** (🪣) first to lay down a background colour before drawing details
- **Stamps** (🌸 🍃 ✦) are great for adding quick texture and decoration
- Adjust **opacity** for softer, layered strokes
- Flowers reset when you close the tab — leave it open to keep your garden growing!
